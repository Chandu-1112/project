Help & Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register a new student?

Navigate to the "Manage Student Information" page. You will find a registration form there. Fill in all the required details, upload a student image, and click "Register Student".

How do I record student attendance?

Go to the "Record Student Attendance" page. Enter the student's name and roll number. A calendar will appear where you can mark days as 'Present' (green) or 'Absent' (red).

Can I view attendance records for past months?

Yes, on the attendance page, you can select different months and years to view the attendance history for a specific student.

What is LUSY and how can she help?

LUSY is your AI assistant, located at the bottom right of the home page. You can ask her any questions related to the system or general queries, and she will provide instant answers by leveraging information from the web (via Google SEO).

Is my data secure?

Yes, we prioritize data security. All data is stored using Firebase Firestore, which provides robust security features. Access to data is controlled based on user authentication.

If you have further questions, feel free to use the LUSY AI assistant on the home page or contact our support team at support@attendancemonitor.com.
